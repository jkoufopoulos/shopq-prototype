Reclaim

Never miss a return window again

Reclaim automatically finds your online purchases in Gmail and tracks return deadlines so you can return items before it's too late.

1

Scan your emails

Reclaim searches your recent Gmail messages for order confirmations and shipping notifications.

2

Extract return dates

AI identifies the merchant, purchase details, and calculates when your return window closes.

3

Track deadlines

See all your return windows in a sidebar right inside Gmail. Get alerts before deadlines expire.

Your privacy matters

  • Your purchase data stays in your browser. We don't store your emails or personal information on our servers.
  • Email content from purchase-related emails is sent to Google's Gemini AI for extraction, then immediately discarded. No data is retained server-side.
  • Personal details (phone numbers, addresses, credit card numbers) are redacted before AI processing.
  • All communication uses encrypted HTTPS connections.
