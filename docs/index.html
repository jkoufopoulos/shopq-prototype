How It Works

Install once. Returns tracked forever.

1

Connect your Gmail

Reclaim finds order confirmations and shipping emails. Only purchase-related messages — nothing else.

2

AI extracts the details

Gemini AI pulls out the merchant, items, order date, and return policy from each email automatically.

3

Watch the countdown

A sidebar in Gmail shows live return deadlines. You'll see them every time you open your inbox.

What you get

Built into Gmail

No new app. No new tab. No new habit. Reclaim adds a sidebar right where you already read email.

Knows retailer policies

Amazon gives you 30 days. Nordstrom gives you 40. Target gives you 90. Reclaim looks up return windows automatically — even when the email doesn't mention one.

Reads messy emails

Order confirmations come in every format imaginable. Gemini AI parses them the way a person would — pulling structured data from marketing HTML and plain text.

Your data stays on your device

Order details live in Chrome's local storage on your machine. When Reclaim processes an email, the content passes through our server to Gemini AI and the result comes right back — nothing is stored, nothing is logged, nothing is sold.

  • All data stored locally in Chrome
  • Server processing is completely transient
  • No ads, no tracking, no data monetization
  • Delete everything with one click
