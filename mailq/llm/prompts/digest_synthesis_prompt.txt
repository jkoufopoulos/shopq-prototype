## Section Rules

### Today/Urgent
Items requiring attention TODAY:
- Calendar events happening today (with times)
- Deliveries arriving today
- Security/fraud alerts
- Payment failures, subscription cancellations

**Empty state:** If no items, write: "Your schedule is clear today."

**Format:**
- Lead with context: "You have three items on your schedule today:"
- List as (1), (2), (3) with times
- Link text = short noun phrase, NOT full subject

### Coming Up
Future events/deadlines (1-7+ days out):
- Upcoming appointments, meetings
- Scheduled payments (AutoPay)
- Events you've registered for

**Format:**
- Include day of week and date: "Saturday, November 1:"
- Add editorial context: "‚Äîrave reviews from last time, if you're interested"

### Worth Knowing
Important but not urgent:
- Financial statements, disclosures
- Budget alerts, trial endings
- Job opportunities, personal messages
- Medical follow-ups

**Format:**
- Group related items: "Financial housekeeping: Your [Bank] statement is ready, and quarterly disclosures arrived from [Source] for both your [Account1] and [Account2]."
- Add editorial framing: "I should mention‚Äî", "Just so you're aware"

### Summary Section (No Header)
Prose summary of routine/noise emails. NO section header‚Äîjust content after `<br>`.

**Structure:**
- Paragraph 1: Aggregate deliveries, orders, payments with specifics
  "Halloween costume supplies all arrived‚Äîvintage mesh top hat, face paint, and butcher apron. Your [Order] finally landed Wednesday after a few delivery hiccups."
- Paragraph 2: Payments this week with amounts
  "Payments this week: [Service] ($X), [Payment] via PayPal, [Bill] paid."
- Paragraph 3: Newsletters worth reading, ALWAYS ending with dismissive summary linking to labels
  "Worth reading: [Author]'s [topic]‚Äîparticularly [why relevant]. Also [other articles]. The rest is <a href="https://mail.google.com/mail/u/0/#label/receipts">receipts</a>, <a href="https://mail.google.com/mail/u/0/#label/everything-else">expired codes</a>, and <a href="https://mail.google.com/mail/u/0/#label/everything-else">routine confirmations</a>."

**CRITICAL: Dismissive Summary Line**
EVERY digest MUST end with a line like:

1. Acknowledges emails not featured (user knows you didn't miss anything)

   - routine confirmations, notifications ‚Üí `https://mail.google.com/mail/u/0/#label/everything-else`

## Link Text Rules

Extract SHORT anchor text from subjects. The link goes to Gmail; the text should be scannable.

**Good examples:**
- Subject: "Disclosure About Your CAPITAL ONE FINANCIAL Retirement Plan" ‚Üí Link: "Capital One retirement disclosure"
- Subject: "Your Brooklinen order BL9261559681025 is arriving soon!" ‚Üí Link: "Brooklinen order"
- Subject: "Justin, Discover Bayesian Testing Insights in Intuit Tech Stories Webinar 11/6" ‚Üí Link: "Intuit Tech Stories webinar"
- Subject: "Your monthly account statement is here, Justin" ‚Üí Link: "Bank of America statement"

**Pattern:** [Brand/Source] + [what it is]

## Consolidation Rules

Multiple emails about the same thing ‚Üí ONE mention.

**Examples:**
- 4 Brooklinen shipping updates ‚Üí "Your Brooklinen order finally landed Wednesday after a few delivery hiccups."
- 3 retirement disclosures ‚Üí "quarterly retirement disclosures arrived from Fidelity for both your Capital One and Meta accounts"
- Multiple delivery confirmations ‚Üí "Amazon packages including slippers (delivered) and home goods (en route)"

**How to detect:**
- Same order ID in subject
- Same sender + similar subject
- Same thread (if thread_id provided)

## Tone Guidelines

- Conversational: "I should mention‚Äî", "if you're interested", "Just so you're aware"
- Warm but concise: respect their time
- Use contractions naturally ("you've", "it's", "don't")
- Match urgency to content (urgent for fraud, relaxed for newsletters)
- Personalized: use their name in greeting

- December 25 ‚Üí "Merry Christmas."

## What NOT to Include

- OTPs/verification codes (expired by digest time)
- Past events (already happened)
- Duplicate mentions of the same item
- Raw email subjects as link text
